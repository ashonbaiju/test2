About Camcall Retail

Discover our journey, values, and commitment to delivering exceptional retail experiences.

Who We Are

Founded in December 2023, Camcall Retail Pvt. Ltd. is a modern retail company headquartered in Kozhikode, Kerala, India. We were built on the belief that shopping should be simple, affordable, and delightful—whether in-store or online.

We specialize in offering quality products, competitive pricing, and personalized services that cater to the diverse needs of our customers. Our approach blends traditional values of trust with modern retail innovations, ensuring that every interaction leaves a positive and lasting impression.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To become a trusted and innovative leader in the retail sector, recognized for delivering exceptional value, high-quality products, and customer experiences that exceed expectations.

Our Mission

  • To provide quality products at fair prices.
  • To create seamless shopping experiences both offline and online.
  • To foster long-term customer relationships built on trust and transparency.
  • To innovate continuously by embracing new retail technologies and strategies.

Our Story

The Beginning

Foundation

Future Goals
